# Cohere Labs Scholars Program 2026 Application

## 🎯 Project Overview

This repository contains all materials for the **Cohere Labs Scholars Program 2026** application, including research papers, video analysis, and application documents. 

**Program Mission**: *"Change where, how and by whom research is done"* through **emerging talent development** and **fundamental research**.

### 🌟 **Key Program Insights from Info Session Analysis**
- **4th Cohort**: Established program welcoming 2026 scholars (January-August, 8 months full-time, paid)
- **Emerging Talent Focus**: **No prior publications preferred** - seeking early career researchers with fresh perspectives
- **Global Impact**: Projects like current scholar Diana's 3B geoclustered models for regional language support
- **Open Science**: Commitment to fundamental research and collaborative learning environment

## 🎯 **Application Strategy Insights from Video Analysis**

### 🌟 **Key Selection Criteria (from Info Session Analysis)**
Based on comprehensive analysis of the official Cohere Labs Scholars Info Session:

**✅ What They Want:**
- **Emerging talent over experience** - No prior publications is actually BETTER
- **Alternative pathways** into AI research (non-traditional backgrounds valued)
- **Global impact focus** - Projects supporting underrepresented communities/languages
- **Fundamental research interest** over applied/commercial applications
- **Collaborative learning mindset** and curiosity-driven approach

**❌ What to Avoid:**
- Overemphasis on prior accomplishments vs. potential
- Generic applications not tailored to Cohere's mission
- Purely technical focus without social impact consideration
- Commercial/industry focus over fundamental research
